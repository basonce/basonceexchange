How to Deposit USDT on BASONCE Exchange

Step 1: Log In

Go to basonce.com and log in to your account. New users can register for free in under 60 seconds.

Step 2: Go to Wallet → Deposit

Tap the Wallet icon in the top navigation, then select "Deposit" from the menu.

Step 3: Select Your Coin

Choose the cryptocurrency you want to deposit — USDT, BTC, ETH, BNB, etc. BASONCE supports 258+ coins.

Step 4: Choose the Network

Select the blockchain network. For USDT, we recommend TRC-20 for the lowest fees (~1 USDT).

Step 5: Copy Your Deposit Address

Copy the wallet address shown on screen (or scan the QR code). This is your personal BASONCE deposit address for that coin/network.

Step 6: Send from Your Wallet

Open your external wallet or exchange and send the crypto to your BASONCE deposit address. Always use the same network.

Step 7: Wait for Confirmation

TRC-20 transactions typically confirm in under 1 minute. Your BASONCE balance will update automatically.

💡 Tip: BASONCE does not charge deposit fees. You only pay the blockchain network fee from your sending wallet.
