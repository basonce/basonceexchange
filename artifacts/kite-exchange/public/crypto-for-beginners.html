Key Terms Every Beginner Must Know

Spot Trading

Buying crypto at the current price. You own the actual asset.

USDT

Stablecoin = $1 USD. Use it as your base currency to buy/sell crypto.

Market Order

Buy or sell immediately at the current best price.

Limit Order

Set a price you want to buy/sell at. Fills when price reaches your target.

Portfolio

All your crypto assets combined. View in the Assets section.

Volatility

Crypto prices can change rapidly. Never invest money you can't afford to lose.
