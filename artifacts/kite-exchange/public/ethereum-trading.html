About Ethereum

Ethereum (ETH) is the world's second-largest cryptocurrency by market cap. It powers the largest ecosystem of decentralized applications, smart contracts, NFTs and DeFi protocols. ETH is the native currency of the Ethereum network and is essential for paying gas fees.

Ethereum's transition to Proof-of-Stake (The Merge) made it significantly more energy-efficient. Ethereum is widely held as a long-term investment and actively traded by millions globally.
